Berkeley Fellowship of Unitarian Universalists

Spirituality and Social Justice in the Heart of Berkeley

The Berkeley Fellowship of Unitarian Universalists is a progressive spiritual community that stands proudly as a beacon for social justice. Join us for Worship Services Sundays at 10:30 a.m.

Our congregation is a diverse community of people who are committed to living our values of justice, compassion, and love. We are a welcoming congregation, and we invite you to join us in our mission to build a more just and compassionate world.

Most of our meetings and events are still online. Our staff is working from home during adjusted office hours (Mon, Tue, Thu, Fri noon - 5PM).

If you need pastoral care or have other concerns, please reach out.
